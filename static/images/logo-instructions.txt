LOGO REPLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS
=============================

To replace the placeholder logo with your actual logo:

1. RECOMMENDED FORMAT: PNG with transparent background
2. RECOMMENDED SIZE: 80x80 pixels (will be automatically scaled)
3. FILE NAME: logo.png
4. LOCATION: /static/images/logo.png

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
- logo.png (Primary - recommended)
- logo.svg (Vector - currently available as placeholder)
- logo.jpg or logo.jpeg (Alternative)

The authentication pages will automatically detect and use your logo.
If no logo is found, they will display the "BF" text placeholder.

CURRENT PLACEHOLDER:
- A beautiful SVG logo with "BF" (Bella Fashion) in gradient colors
- Matches your website's branding colors (#FF2BAC, #FF6BCE, #FF9ED6)
- Professional and modern design

TO REPLACE:
1. Save your logo as "logo.png" in the /static/images/ folder
2. The pages will automatically use your new logo
3. No code changes needed!

DESIGN RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Use your brand colors
- Keep it simple and readable at small sizes  
- Ensure good contrast
- Consider a square or circular design for best appearance
